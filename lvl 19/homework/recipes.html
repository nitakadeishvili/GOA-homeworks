Khachapuri Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 250ml warm water
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 400g Imeretian cheese (or similar)

Preparation Steps:

1. Mix flour, salt, water, and oil to form dough.

2. Let the dough rest for 30 minutes.

3. Roll out the dough and place the cheese inside.

4. Cook on both sides in a pan or bake in the oven.

Fun Facts:

There are several types of khachapuri, such as Adjarian, Imeretian, and Megrelian.

You can use alternative cheeses like sulguni or a mix of cheeses for variation.
